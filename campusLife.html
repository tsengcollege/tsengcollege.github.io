The CSUN campus is modern and state-of-the-art. Facilities include the University Library with approximately 1.4 million books and periodicals; high-tech scientific and computer labs; sports, athletic and recreational centers; an on-campus bank and a full-service Student Health Center. On-campus housing is available. All housing units are apartment style with free wireless Internet connection.

CSUN offers hundreds of on-campus events - including concerts, theatrical performances, films, guest speakers and sports competitions - year-round. The CSUN campus occupies 356 acres and is within easy walking distance of many restaurants, supermarkets and shops.

With the activities available, students enjoy countless opportunities to meet and interact with university students. CSUN provides a variety of activities - educational, cultural and social - including student clubs; musical, theatrical and sports events; enrichment activities hosted by various academic departments; and presentations by distinguished faculty and prominent figures in politics, science and the arts.
