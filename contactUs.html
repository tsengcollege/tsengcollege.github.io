Graduate and Professional Education and Student Services (GPE)

programs@csun.edu
(818) 677-3332
GPE Program Management Teams:
Program management (PM) teams facilitate student success as the main point of contact for all prospective and current student questions and needs throughout their journey at CSUN, from application to graduation. Please click the button below to find the program related to your program of interest/study.
PM Virtual Appointments

Admissions, Registration and Client Services (ARC)

collexl@csun.edu
(818) 677-2504
The ARC team is the point of contact for various questions regarding admission and registration information for programs offered through the Tseng College. The department provides application, registration and student account services and technical systems support. The ARC department also provides I-20s and immigration guidance to international students who are enrolled in programs that are offered through the Tseng College.
Schedule A Virtual Appointment

Finance - Student Account Services (FIN)

tsengfinance@csun.edu
(818) 677-4174
Student Account Services:
The Student Account Services team supports students on managing the student account's billing and payment. The team also reminds students of account balances, explains the charges in the student's accounts, and answers questions related to received payments. The team provides services to the students as well as their sponsoring agencies.

Please contact the department directly to speak to an account specialist.

Financial Aid (FA)

tsengfinance@csun.edu
(818) 677-4174
The Tseng College has a dedicated team of financial aid professionals who work with the many students who receive financial aid to pay for tuition and other costs of attendance. Financial Aid counselors are assigned to specific programs to better assist prospective and current students with all financial aid related questions, requests and needs.

Please email exlfinaid@csun.edu to schedule a virtual appointment.

University Access (UA)

universityaccess@csun.edu
(818) 677-6984
The University Access Programs unit is responsible for the various “extra-term” programs available to both current CSUN students and visiting members of the community. Our programs include Winter Term, Open University, and Continuing Education Unit (CEU) and non-credit workshops. Unit staff is available to provide general support and guidance on program processes and enrollment.Please email universityaccess@csun.edu to schedule a virtual appointment.
